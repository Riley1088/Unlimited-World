Swords & Forge

🗨 How to Obtain Swords

  • Pumpkin chest in each world
  • Quests (Worlds 1–5)
  • Raids

⚒ Sword Fusion

Each tier requires 3 swords from the previous tier:
Normal → Shine → Godly → Mystery

🔥 Forge (Map 2)

Use Souls to upgrade your sword's multiplier for Power Per Swing.
Souls drop from Superbosses and Tower Map 2.
